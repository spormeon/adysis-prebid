      keeping items out of landfill since 2004

      Founded 2nd November, 2010. 55970 current freeglers. More stats or stories

      Our basic rules:

      All items must be offered and requested for free. 

      All items must be legal to give away.

      Be polite to all members and volunteers.

      No Wanted posts for animals.

      No offers of cats, dogs, rabbits etc. Fish are ok to offer.

      No selling, swaps, loans or advice.

      How Does It Work? - It's simple.

      Post a message offering an item.

      Other people see it and reply to you.

      Choose who to give it to and they collect.

      Select My Posts, and mark the item as Taken

      You have decluttered, made someone's day and saved an item from landfill.

      We hope that you have fun Freegling with us.

      If you do have a problem with it please tell us - we listen. 

      We are on Facebook and Twitter @BristolFreegle
