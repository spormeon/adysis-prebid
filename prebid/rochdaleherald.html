You had some very fine people on both sides, Trump tells D-Day veterans

By
Quentin D Fortesqueue
-
June 5, 2019
2768
Facebook
Twitter
Google+
Pinterest
WhatsApp

    Donald Trump has told D-Day veterans that there were very fine people on both sides of the battles to control the Normandy Beaches during World War Two during a press conference in Portsmouth today.

    Speaking to reporters he pointed out that the Allied forces, or Alt-Left as he prefers to call them, came charging up the beaches and launched a completely unprovoked assault against the Alt-Right.

    “Those guys charging up the beaches didn’t even have a permit to be there. There was blame on both sides.”

    “Sure there were quite a lot of Nazis manning machine gun turrets. Sure you had a lot of white supremacists there, but there were some very fine people there too.”

    D-Day veterans and the Queen were heard to mutter “For fuck’s sake.”
